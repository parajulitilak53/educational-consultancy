PTE Exam - General Facts
  • PTE Academic is a computer-based exam carefully evaluated by a specially designed computer system.
  • Introduced by Pearson PLC Group, PTE Academic carries the endorsement of the Graduate Management Admission Council (GMAC).
  • Scores are rated on a 10 to 90 scale according to the Global Scale of English, covering three sections: Speaking & Writing, Reading, and Listening.
  • The Speaking and Writing section extends from 54 to 67 minutes.
  • The Reading section has a duration of 29 to 30 minutes.
  • The Listening section spans 30 to 43 minutes.
  • Additional scores are provided for enabling skills, including grammar, oral fluency, pronunciation, spelling, vocabulary, and written discourse.
  • In Nepal, the cost of PTE Academic is NPR 25,333, equivalent to USD 190.
  • Typically, test results are accessible within five days.
  • The validity of the PTE Academic Scorecard extends for two years from the date of the exam.
