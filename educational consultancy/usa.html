Minimum Qualification Requirements for USA

Diploma/Advanced Diploma (VET) program
Academic Requirements English Proficiency Requirements Exceptions
GPA 2.7 NEB (10+2) Overall Score of 5.5 in IELTS with no band less than 5.0 or PTE Academic 42 A diploma in Nursing usually has higher IELTS requirements. A few TAFE colleges may have higher IELTS requirements.
Pathways
Academic Requirements English Proficiency Requirements Exceptions
GPA 2.7 NEB (10+2) or equivalent Overall Score of 5.5 in IELTS with no band less than 5.0 or PTE Academic 42 Diploma in Nursing has higher IELTS requirements and also other Pathway programs at TAFE may have higher IELTS requirements.
Bachelor degree
Academic Requirements English Proficiency Requirements Exceptions
GPA 2.8 NEB (10+2) or equivalent Overall Score of 6.0 in IELTS with no band less than 5.5 or PTE Academic 50 A few Bachelor programs at certain specific TAFE may need higher IELTS 6.5 (6.0) and also Bachelor programs in Nursing.
Master degree
Academic Requirements English Proficiency Requirements Exceptions
Bachelor’s degree with some industry experience Overall Score of 6.5 in IELTS with no band less than 6.0 or PTE Academic 58 A few universities may accept students with an IELTS score of 6.0 (5.5) or IELTS 6.0 (6.0) for certain programs. Programs such as TESOL or Nursing require English proficiency at the C1 level (IELTS 7.0 with no band less than 7.0).
